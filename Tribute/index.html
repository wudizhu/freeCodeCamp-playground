Loch an Eilein

A beautiful lake bank in north Scotland.

Following the river ness to the north, you will find arguably the most famous lake of the Scotland - loch ness. However after visiting it several times, I find it to be rather liveless and lack of characters. So we set out to find some new lakes which we can show our guests from aboard.

Cairngorms national park resides just east of loch ness. Discovering a new lake in this large lake network should be fairly easy. When we were driving aimless on the road, We encountered a signboard with a strange name - loch an eilein.

It was a small lake with an irregular shape. The lake bank was not disrupted by man-made construtions. The rough stones and soft ground made it quite challenging and exicting to walk along.

If you have time, you can read more about it on Wikipedia .
